Jamie Oliver Chorizo and Tomato salad

Chorizo
Salad Onions
Salad Tomato
Cherry Tomato
Garlic
Sherry Vinegar
Olive Oil Extra Virgin


Chop the tomatoes and salad onions and mix in a bowl. Drizzle with olive oil and sherry vinegar

Remove the casing from the chorizo and slice. Fry for a few minutes and then add the garlic. Before it turns brown remove from the heat and add sherry vinegar.

Serve on top of the salad.
